Welcome To My Site!

*****This site is a work in progress. Please enjoy!*****

I am a builder and designer of web applications. A strong believer in the never ending process of learning especially from those around me. I am passionate about the use of technology to solve everyday problems. I love not just using but experiencing technology. I am a fan of open source software. I feel that we should individually struggle with but collectively solve problems.

I am always happy to see development through the iterative learning process derived from "The Lean Startup Principles." I have and eye for customer service and firmly believe that a product's value is derived from a user's implicit and explicit utility.

I am a proponent of health and fitness stemming from my studies in Medicine and Kinesiology. You will find me pondering the complexities of physics not attempting to solve the problem of existence but simply attempting to understand The Universe in which we live.
